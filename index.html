Beta Scoring Methodology

Each signal is scored across five dimensions. State scores are the sum of all entry scores. Risk tiers are assigned based on that total.

Grid −10 to +10
Capacity, reliability, and interconnection
Regulatory −10 to +10
Policy, permitting, tariffs, zoning, incentives
Water −10 to 0
Scarcity, restrictions, community opposition
Clean Energy −5 to +5
Renewable access, PPA market, RPS strength
Saturation −5 to +5
Development pressure and cost socialization
